Delete Your Account & Data

You can permanently delete your Expnz account and all associated data directly from within the app. This action is irreversible.

How to delete your account

Follow these steps in the Expnz app
1
Open the Expnz app on your device
2
Tap the Settings icon from the home screen
3
Scroll down and tap Account
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm when prompted
Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased immediately and cannot be recovered.

What happens to your data

Data deletion summary
Account & authentication data Deleted immediately
All bills and expense records Deleted immediately
Subscription & purchase history Deleted immediately
Push notification token (FCM) Deleted immediately
Anonymous analytics data Deleted immediately
iCloud / Google Drive backup Delete manually from your device
Your bill data is stored locally on your device and in your own iCloud or Google Drive — Expnz never stores your financial data on our servers. Your cloud backup files can be deleted from your device's iCloud or Google Drive settings at any time.
